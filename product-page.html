Description

Introduction
W.L. Weller Special Reserve Wheated Bourbon 750ML — 45% ABV — USA Step into the legacy of Kentucky bourbon with this exceptional wheated expression, renowned for its smooth, mellow profile and rich heritage. Crafted in the heart of America’s bourbon country, each sip reveals a harmonious blend of caramel, honey, and soft vanilla, all elevated by the distinctive use of wheat in the mash bill. This thoughtful substitution imparts an approachable softness and gentle sweetness, making it both an inviting introduction for newcomers and a cherished favorite for seasoned enthusiasts. Elegance, tradition, and meticulous attention to detail shine through in every pour—an ode to the artistry of American whiskey making.

Highlights
W.L. Weller Special Reserve Wheated Bourbon is a distinguished American whiskey, crafted with a unique wheated mash bill that sets it apart in the bourbon category. Bottled at 45% ABV, this 750ML offering is sought after for its smooth, approachable character and premium quality. As a representative of the USA’s renowned whiskey tradition, it appeals to both collectors and enthusiasts who appreciate a softer, well-rounded bourbon profile. Experience a classic expression that showcases the elevated craftsmanship associated with wheated bourbons, making it a staple for any fine spirits collection.

Tasting Notes
The appearance of W.L. Weller Special Reserve Wheated Bourbon is marked by a warm, inviting amber hue in the glass. Upon nosing, this whiskey offers a balanced bouquet, with gentle notes that evoke a mellow sweetness and a subtle undertone of oak. The palate delivers a smooth, velvety texture, highlighting the signature softness imparted by the wheat in the mash bill. Flavors develop gracefully, presenting a harmonious blend of sweet and subtle spice, without overwhelming the senses. The finish is pleasingly gentle and lingers with a mellow warmth, making each sip both satisfying and approachable for aficionados and newcomers alike. This bourbon demonstrates the appeal of carefully crafted wheated whiskey and its capacity for depth and refinement.
